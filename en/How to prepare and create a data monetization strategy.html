How to prepare and create a data monetization strategy:

Begin by comprehensively understanding the data your company generates. Conduct an inventory of business processes that naturally produce data. A data monetization strategy is an executable plan for extracting "value" from data and converting it into revenue-generating opportunities. The most successful data companies follow these 5 steps to developing a successful, scalable data monetization strategy: parsing, products, pricing, platform.

To prepare for data monetization in 4 steps:

  1. Gain buy-in from stakeholders.
  2. Assess existing data and determine future collection needs.
  3. Decide your target audience for the monetized data.
  4. Establish a clear plan on how to extract value from the data.

Three stages of the data monetization lifecycle process:

  1. Create: Raw data is accessed and assembled into data products.
  2. Serve: Data products are delivered to the intended audience.
  3. Optimize: Continuously improve and refine the data monetization strategy based on feedback and results.

To take advantage of data monetization internally, review your data strategy to ensure alignment with the overall business goals. Improving work processes, wrapping products with data insights, and selling information offerings are key ways to monetize data effectively.

Developing a broad and shared understanding of data monetization within the organization is essential for success. This includes educating all stakeholders on the benefits and opportunities of data monetization and ensuring that everyone is aligned towards the common goal of extracting value from data.
